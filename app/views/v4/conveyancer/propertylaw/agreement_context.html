You can now agree and sign your sales and transfer agreements

You'll need your Yoti ID to do this.
  1. Your sales contract is your written agreement with the seller which sets out the terms of the sale.
    It specifies the rights and duties of you and the seller. It needs to be signed by all parties before it can be exchanged and become legally binding.
  2. When you've signed your mortgage agreement, it confirms that you're happy to proceed based on the terms of your mortgage offer.
    Upon completion of your property sale, the signed mortgage agreement is legally binding.
  3. HM Land Registry need to register your new property in your name. Once this is done, ownership of the property will be legally transferred to you from the seller.
