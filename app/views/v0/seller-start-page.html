Sell land or property

Use this service to:

  • confirm that you own the land or property you want to sell

    • - estate agent
    • - conveyancer

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

You'll need to sign in with Identify to use this service.
If you haven't used Identify before, it'll take about 15 minutes to set up an account.

We'll retain and may share your personal details for fraud prevention and detection purposes. We may also carry out third party checks in relation to the information supplied. For further information please view our personal information charter.
